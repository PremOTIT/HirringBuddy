the part nobody told you about yet

You also have a Telegram bot 🧸

A little note before we start — this bot is new and still learning its own footing, same as any of us on a first day. If something feels clunky or a message doesn't land quite right, that's alright. Be gentle with it, and it'll take good care of you back. 💛

HiringBuddy isn't just the app on your home screen — there's a second little helper living in Telegram, made for scheduling interviews and getting gentle reminders without opening anything at all.

🎀 two halves, one buddy

How the two pieces fit together

You don't need to choose — they're meant to work side by side, each doing what it's best at.

📱
The HiringBuddy app
Your dashboards. The one you screen-share in interviews. Lives on your home screen.
💬
The HiringBuddy bot
Lives inside Telegram. For scheduling interviews, logging outcomes, and getting reminders — just by texting.

Two separate spaces, each good at something different.

One honest note: they don't share candidates with each other yet — someone added in the bot won't show up in the app, and the other way round. Pick whichever feels natural for the moment.

🐝 finding it

Getting started, step by step

1

Open the link you were sent

You'll have gotten a Telegram invite link alongside this doc — tapping it opens a chat with the bot directly, the same way any Telegram contact would.

2

Say hello

Send /start — this is the only "setup" step. The bot introduces itself and shows you what it can do.

3

Just talk to it like texting a colleague

No app to learn, no menus to hunt through — everything happens through short messages, the same way you'd message anyone else.
