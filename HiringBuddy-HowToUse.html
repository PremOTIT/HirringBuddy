🐻 step one

Installing it

HiringBuddy installs onto your phone or laptop like a real app — no app store needed, just your browser.

1

Open the link

Tap the link you were sent — opens in Chrome, completely normal, just like any website.

2

Add it to your home screen

Tap the ⋮ (three dots) → "Add to Home Screen" or "Install app."

3

Find the little bear

A teddy bear icon now sits on your home screen — tap it any time, no need to find the link again.

🔐 step two

Setting your PIN

The very first time you open it, choose a 4-digit PIN to keep things private.

the very first screen you'll see
Pick a PIN you'll actually remember — there's no "forgot PIN" button. If you forget it, the fix means starting fresh, unless you've signed in to Drive backup already. 💛

➕ step four

Adding a candidate

This is what you'll do most — just a few taps.

1

Tap the orange +

Always sitting bottom-right, wherever you are.

the form that opens when you tap +
2

Fill in what you know

Name and role are the basics — everything else is optional.

3

Tap "Save candidate"

That's it — every dashboard updates instantly.

☁️ step seven

Signing in & backup

This is the part that means you'll never lose your work.

1

Sign in with Google

Found in the menu — use your own personal account.

2

Approve access

Creates a private backup file only this app can see.

3

It backs up on its own

A few seconds after every change, and again every few hours.

New phone? Install, set your PIN, sign in with the same account — everything comes back automatically. 💛
